Web Developer Intern

Join our engineering team to build modern, scalable web applications that power intelligent systems.

  • Develop responsive UIs using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and frameworks like React or Vue.
  • Integrate with backend APIs (Node.js, Python, or PHP).
  • Ensure performance, accessibility, and cross-browser compatibility.
  • Contribute to debugging, testing, and iterative improvements.
  • Propose and implement architectural and UI enhancements.

UI/UX Designer Intern

Design user-centered experiences across web and mobile platforms aligned with the OneONN design language.

  • Create wireframes, UI mockups, and interactive prototypes using Figma or Adobe XD.
  • Collaborate with developers and researchers to deliver seamless user interfaces.
  • Conduct usability testing and refine designs based on insights.
  • Ensure visual consistency across all interfaces and materials.
  • Work with branding and design systems for scalable UI development.

Research Intern

Support teams with research that informs strategic decisions, product direction, and user understanding.

  • Conduct market and user research, competitor analysis, and persona development.
  • Use tools like Excel, Google Analytics, and Tableau to analyze and present findings.
  • Assist in user testing and hypothesis validation.
  • Prepare structured reports and internal presentations.
  • Contribute to research documentation for long-term insights and planning.

What We Offer

  • Real-world experience with measurable impact.
  • Mentorship across engineering, design, and research disciplines.
  • Flexible remote/hybrid work options.
  • A portfolio of tangible projects to showcase professionally.
  • Inclusive, innovation-driven culture that values contribution and growth.

Who Should Apply

  • Students or recent graduates in Computer Science, Design, Data Science, or related fields.
  • Proficient in relevant tools such as JavaScript frameworks, Figma, Excel, or equivalent.
  • Strong communication and collaborative skills.
  • Curious, proactive, and aligned with OneONN’s mission.
  • Available for a 3–6 month internship starting August 2025.

How to Apply

Please submit your application by July 15, 2025 to careers@oneonn.com with:

  • Portfolio or samples (GitHub, design files, or research reports)
